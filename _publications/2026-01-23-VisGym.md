VisGym evaluates multimodal agents in multi-step visual interaction settings where agents must integrate perception, memory, and action over long horizons. It includes 17 diverse environments with controllable difficulty, input representation, planning horizon, and feedback, and exposes failure modes in long-context use, visual grounding, and interactive planning.
