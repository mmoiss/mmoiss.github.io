Contrastive image-text models often prioritize high-level semantics over fine-grained visual understanding. TULIP incorporates generative data augmentation, image-image and text-text contrastive objectives, and image/text reconstruction regularization to improve vision-language alignment while preserving detailed visual features. The approach improves zero-shot classification, fine-grained recognition, and multimodal reasoning benchmarks.
