Parallel reasoning benefits from sampling multiple candidate solutions, but selecting the correct candidate is a central bottleneck. V1 uses pairwise self-verification and uncertainty-guided tournament ranking at inference time, plus an RL framework that trains one model as both generator and pairwise verifier.
