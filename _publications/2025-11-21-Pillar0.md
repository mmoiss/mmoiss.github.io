Pillar-0 is a radiology foundation model for volumetric CT and MRI scans, evaluated across hundreds of clinical findings. Together with RATE, a scalable label extraction framework, Pillar-0 advances broad radiology representation learning and supports downstream tasks including external validation, cancer risk prediction, and brain hemorrhage detection.
