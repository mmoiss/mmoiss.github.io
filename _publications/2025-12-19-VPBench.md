Visually prompted VLM benchmarks can conflate visual reasoning ability with sensitivity to superficial presentation details. This work shows that marker style, dataset size, and even JPEG compression can reorder model leaderboards, then introduces VPBench, a larger visually prompted benchmark with multiple marker variants for more stable evaluation.
